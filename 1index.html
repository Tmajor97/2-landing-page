About us

In Emerald Fitness club, you will be cared for by industry leading experts wherever you are. Our Gym team have specific qualifications and experience to provide an unrivalled experience for all members whether it’s your first time in the gym or you’re setting yourself a new deadlifting target. Our staff are passionate about creating a fun and sociable atmosphere in the Gym to ensure exercise is a choice not a chore. The Gym experts will offer all new members a programme and be on hand to continue providing help and advice for a successful and satisfying workout. As a member, you can also take part in challenges aimed at everyone from beginner to elite athlete, attend health and fitness workshops or get event training advice.

Facilities

The gym at Emerald Fitness club offers an unrivalled experience with a huge range of Technogym equipment. Covering two levels, the upper floor contains a huge range of cardiovascular and resistance machines as well as an area for functional training and stretching. The lower floor has an extensive range of free weights, Olympic lifting platforms, cable machines and cardio machines for warming up. The gym overlooks the 50 metre swimming pool and has a light, spacious feel. Please note, all gym members will need to bring a towel on every visit to the gym. You can buy a Sport & Fitness sweat towel from reception for £8.
